THE GRAND OPENING

We are thrilled to invite you to the grand opening of our brand-new museum on May 14th, 2025! Join us as we celebrate a journey through art, history, and culture in a space designed to inspire and connect. Be among the first to explore our captivating exhibits, meet the curators, and enjoy a day filled with creativity, discovery, and community. Mark your calendar and be part of this unforgettable moment. we can’t wait to welcome you!

ACTIVITIES

The grand opening day will be filled with exciting activities for all ages. Guests will enjoy guided tours through our stunning galleries, featuring both permanent collections and special exhibits curated just for this launch. Art lovers will have the chance to meet some of the featured artists and hear the stories behind their work. Throughout the day, live performances—including music, dance, and spoken word—will bring the museum’s spaces to life, creating an immersive and unforgettable cultural experience.

In addition to the exhibits and performances, there will be interactive workshops where visitors can try their hand at painting, sculpture, or digital art creation. Children will enjoy a dedicated Kids’ Corner with fun educational games, storytelling sessions, and creative crafts. Delicious food and refreshments will be available from local vendors, and a ribbon-cutting ceremony will officially mark the opening of the museum. It's a celebration of art, community, and inspiration you won’t want to miss!
